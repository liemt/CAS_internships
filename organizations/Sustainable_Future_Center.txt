Sustainable Future Center

About the Organization
Sustainable Future Center seeks to help the community become aware of alternative ways to get human needs met while improving the earth's ability to support life. This empowerment strengthens both the individual and the community.

Internship Details
Business Type: Nonprofit
Additional Description: The SFC is a beacon of knowledge about the sustainability efforts in Knoxville and have become a local resource over the years with their many involvements and projects in the Knoxville community. Students with interest in sustainability and have projects or research they would like to focus on during this internship are preferred.
Long-term Internship: Yes to any long-term for internships. Prefer full academic year.
Short-term Internship/Shadowing: Open to providing on-site classroom space, access to on-site property for various studies, or short on-site tours.
Paid/Unpaid: Unpaid
Research & Course Project Opportunities: Open to research solely focused on sustainability or natural sciences. Course Opportunities: Open to students and faculty utilizing on-site classroom, library, property. Open to giving on-site presentations that align with course curriculum. Open to mentoring or being a resource for student projects. Open to hosting student/dept/college orchestrated events for other non-profits or workshops.
Divisional Interest: SS & NSM. Specific Interest in: Geography & Sustainability department. EEPS department. EEB department.
