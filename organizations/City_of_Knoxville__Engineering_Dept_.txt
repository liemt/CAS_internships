City of Knoxville- Engineering Dept.

About the Organization
The Engineering Department performs all engineering and technical services for city departments, including design work and contract administration for streets, bridges, sidewalks, drainage and other public works projects.

Internship Details
Business Type: Government
Additional Description: The City of Knoxville's Engineering department is split into four sections of: civil engineering, stormwater engineering, development services engineering, and transportation engineering. The selected interns will have a chance to participate in a one:one mentorship with an involved staff member, from a designated section, who can provide them with realistic insight to the career field and professional expectations.
