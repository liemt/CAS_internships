East TN Children's Hospital- Child Life Program

About the Organization
East Tennessee Children's Hospital offers a Child Life Internship program, where college students can work beside child life specialists in the hospital setting.

Internship Details
Business Type: Program
Additional Description: The Child Life program focuses on children minimize their stress and provide effective coping skills in regard to the medical challenges they may face. This would be a great place for students who are interested in child-focused careers or medical fields.
Long-term Internship: N/A
Short-term Internship/Shadowing: Open to short-term internships. Previous Child Life volunteer experiences is preferred.
Paid/Unpaid: Unpaid
Research & Course Project Opportunities: Open to anything focused on child life, family studies, child development, ethics, and health. Course Opportunities: Open to guest-speak with specific courses/Pre-Health students.
Divisional Interest: Pre-Professional & NSM & SS
