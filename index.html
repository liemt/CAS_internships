Welcome Message from the Associate Dean for Academic Programs

Dear Students,

Welcome to the CAS Academic Programs' Corner! This site is your go-to hub for opportunities, resources, and support to help you thrive both academically and personally.

As the Associate Dean for Academic Programs, I am proud to work with our incredible students, faculty, and staff to create meaningful experiences that go beyond departmental boundaries. This website reflects our commitment to your holistic development, providing access to research opportunities, career resources, scholarships, wellness support, and more—all designed to help you achieve your goals.

Here, you’ll discover ways to connect with interdisciplinary programs, explore study abroad, engage in community-building initiatives, and pursue your passions. It’s not just about academics; it’s about empowering you to grow as a person and a professional.

Explore the resources available here and make the most of what the College of Arts and Sciences has to offer. If you have questions or need guidance, don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here to support you every step of the way.

Dr. Liem Tran
