TN Nonprofit Network

About the Organization
Tennessee Nonprofit Network serves as the statewide nonprofit association for Tennessee. The organization provides training and technical assistance, connections to resources, and sector-wide research and public policy support to nonprofits in Tennessee and those that serve Tennessee residents.
