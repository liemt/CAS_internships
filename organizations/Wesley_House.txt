Wesley House

About the Organization
Wesley House is committed to fostering and maintaining a work environment where diversity, equity, and inclusion are fully integrated into everything we do for the benefit of our employees, families, and community members we are called to serve.

Internship Details
Business Type: Nonprofit
Additional Description: Wesley House focuses their efforts on youth development, senior support, and providing emergent needs to the local community. They host afterschool and summer programs for Knoxville kids in grades K-8. For senior support, once a week, they assist with games and activities that actively engage with attendees at senior community centers or facilities. With addressing emergent needs, they provide support and food through a pantry that has local grocery store & garden partners. They also manage a community store that is supplied by donations and managed by volunteer involvement.
Long-term Internship: Yes to any long-term for internships. Prefer full academic year.
Short-term Internship/Shadowing: Open to short-term internships. Open to shadowing.
Paid/Unpaid: Unpaid
Research & Course Project Opportunities: N/A Course Opportunities: Open to students assisting with their senior support program, food pantry, or community store. Open to aligning with a course curriculum to implement surveys, data, studies, etc.
Divisional Interest: AH & NSM & SS & Pre-Professional & IDP (all)
