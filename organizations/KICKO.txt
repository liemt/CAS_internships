KICKO

About the Organization
The mission of KICKO is to transform our inner-city by equipping and empowering its children to fully realize their potential, while having Christian-based values.

Internship Details
Business Type: Nonprofit
Additional Description: The Knoxville Inner City Kids Outreach program that focuses on bringing faith and positivity to children living in hopeless situations. The program mentors provide security and stability to children who need accountability from older/adult figures. There are sixteen KICKO communities throughout the Knoxville area & are interested in college student support in their efforts.
